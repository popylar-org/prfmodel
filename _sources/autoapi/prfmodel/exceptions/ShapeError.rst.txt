prfmodel.exceptions.ShapeError
==============================

.. py:exception:: prfmodel.exceptions.ShapeError(arg_name: str, arg_shape: tuple[int, Ellipsis], requirement: str)



   Exception raised when an argument has an invalid shape.

   :param arg_name: Name of the argument.
   :type arg_name: str
   :param arg_shape: Shape of the argument.
   :type arg_shape: tuple of int
   :param requirement: Description of the shape requirement that was violated (e.g. ``"must have at least 2 dimensions"``).
   :type requirement: str
